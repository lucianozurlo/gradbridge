Jen O'Donald

Chief Executive Officer

Jen is a seasoned financial services executive with over 25 years of experience in consumer credit, analytics, and student lending.

As an executive at Sallie Mae, she led product, marketing, business development and analytics. Jen launched new credit products at large companies and early-stage startups and built a consulting practice helping companies launch new revenue generating products.

As both a former student lending leader and a parent of college-aged kids, Jen founded GradBridge to solve a critical gap in the market: helping capable students access the funding they need to graduate and succeed.
